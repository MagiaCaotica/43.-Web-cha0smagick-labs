Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Introduction

Cha0smagick Labs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data. Our core philosophy is privacy-first. All our Android apps operate 100% offline with zero data collection. This website uses the following:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns (page views, geographic regions, device types). This data is anonymized and cannot identify individual users. Google Privacy Policy
  • Visitor Map: Our interactive map shows approximate visitor locations based on IP geolocation. No precise location data is stored or transmitted.
  • Visitor Counter: A simple localStorage counter that tracks the number of unique visits to your browser. No personal data is transmitted.

Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Uses cookies to distinguish unique users and track session information. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • Google Translate: The language translation widget may set cookies for language preference.
  • No tracking cookies are used for advertising, profiling, or cross-site tracking.

Third-Party Services

Our website integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (analytics)
  • Google Translate (language translation)
  • Leaflet.js + CartoDB (open-source map visualization)
  • Google Fonts (typography)

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We do not share personal data with these services beyond what is necessary for their operation.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion: Request removal of your data.
  • Right to opt out: You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at magiacaoticapractica@gmail.com.

Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS. We implement security headers (CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options) to protect against common web vulnerabilities. Our Android apps process all data locally on your device with no cloud transmission.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
