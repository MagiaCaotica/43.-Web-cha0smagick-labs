About Cha0smagick Labs

Our History

Cha0smagick Labs was born in 2026 as a natural evolution of the Magia del Caos Práctica group, a community dedicated to the study and practice of Chaos Magick on Telegram and YouTube. What began as a space for knowledge exchange transformed into a development laboratory for cybermagick technology.

Founded by Frater Alek0s, our collective brings together cybermancers, programmers and occultists who share a common vision: to digitize the arcane.

Our Mission

To digitize esoteric knowledge and create high-performance spiritual tools that fuse Chaos Magick with advanced algorithms. We empower the modern practitioner with precision, efficacy and accessibility.

Our Vision

To stand as the global vanguard of Techno-Sorcery, preserving ancient wisdom through code while building a future where reality hacking is accessible, professional and ethical.

What We Do

  • Chaos Magick Apps for Android: We develop esoteric applications that work 100% offline, including sigil generators, rune oracles, lunar phase calculators and digital grimoires.
  • PDF Books & Manuals: We publish digital grimoires on magical servitors, chaos hunter runes, magical ouija and lycanthropic transformation.
  • Community: We maintain an active community on Telegram and YouTube where we share knowledge, rituals and developments.

The Team

Our team is led by Frater Alek0s, founder of the Magia del Caos Práctica group and author of multiple digital grimoires. We have a team of anonymous mages who act as testers, collaborators and advisors in the development of our cybermagick technology.

Our Principles

  • Total Privacy: All our apps work offline and do not collect personal data.
  • Knowledge Preservation: We translate ancient traditions into digital formats for future generations.
  • Accessibility: We democratize esoteric knowledge through intuitive interfaces.
  • Innovation: We combine ancestral wisdom with cutting-edge technology.
