Frequently Asked Questions

What is the silver cord?

A luminous, elastic thread described in occult literature connecting the physical body to the astral body during OBEs. It ensures the projector can always return to their body.

Is the silver cord always visible?

No. Many experienced projectors never see it. Its function is connection, not observation. Some believe it becomes visible only when consciously perceived.

Can the silver cord break?

In occult literature, breaking is associated with physical death. In normal OBE practice, the cord cannot break — it is a continuous field of awareness that expands and contracts elastically.
