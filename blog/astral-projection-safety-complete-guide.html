Astral Projection Safety: Complete Guide to Safe Out-of-Body Travel (2026)

By Frater Alek0s • July 16, 2026 • 12 min read

Safe Astral Travel

Astral projection is a natural human ability practiced safely for millennia. However, respect and preparation are essential. This guide covers the ten most important safety protocols.

Before Projection

  • Ground yourself physically — eat a meal, exercise, connect with nature
  • Set clear protective intentions
  • Choose a quiet, safe environment
  • Ensure you will not be disturbed
  • Affirm your ability to return safely

During Projection

  • Stay calm — excitement can snap you back to your body
  • Maintain awareness of your physical body location
  • If you encounter fear-inducing phenomena, banish or return calmly
  • Do not travel too far from your body in early sessions

After Projection

  • Ground thoroughly — eat, touch physical objects, move your body
  • Journal your experience immediately
  • Hydrate — astral travel can be energetically depleting
  • Review any fear responses and address them before next session

Frequently Asked Questions

Is astral projection dangerous?

With proper precautions, astral projection is safe. Always ground before and after, set protective intentions, and maintain awareness of your physical body. Avoid when under substances or emotional distress.

What is grounding and why is it important?

Grounding reconnects you fully with your physical body after an OBE. Methods include eating, touching natural objects, physical exercise, and affirmations. Grounding prevents disorientation and psychic fatigue.

Can I get stuck outside my body?

No. The silver cord or continuous awareness always connects you to your body. At most, you may feel briefly disoriented. Affirming "I return to my body now" is sufficient to re-enter.
