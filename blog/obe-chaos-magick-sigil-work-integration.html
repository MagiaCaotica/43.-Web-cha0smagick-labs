Frequently Asked Questions

How does OBE enhance chaos magick?

In the OBE state, you operate directly on the astral plane where sigils and servitors exist. This allows more direct manipulation of magical constructs and more powerful charging than from the physical state alone.

Can I create servitors during astral projection?

Yes. The astral plane is ideal for servitor creation because you can directly shape energy into form. Many advanced practitioners create their most powerful servitors while projecting.

Is OBE-based magic more effective?

Many practitioners report that magical work performed during OBEs manifests faster and more powerfully. The direct access to the astral plane bypasses the resistance of physical consciousness.
