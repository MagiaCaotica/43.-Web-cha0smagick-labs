How Brainwave Entrainment Works

Brainwave entrainment synchronizes your brain's electrical activity to external rhythmic stimuli. Listening to binaural beats at specific frequencies shifts your brain toward that frequency.

Brainwave States

Delta (1-4 Hz): deep sleep awareness. Theta (4-8 Hz): REM sleep, hypnagogia — primary lucid dreaming induction. Alpha (8-12 Hz): relaxed awareness for WBTB. Gamma (30-100 Hz): dream clarity enhancement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do binaural beats really work?

Yes. A 2019 study found participants using theta binaural beats had 23% higher lucid dreaming frequency. The mechanism is brainwave entrainment guiding the brain into the theta state (4-8 Hz).

What frequency is best?

Theta (4-8 Hz) for the hypnagogic state. Low theta (4-5 Hz) for relaxation. High theta (6-8 Hz) for REM awareness. Gamma (40 Hz) enhances dream clarity.

Do I need headphones?

Yes for binaural beats. Stereo headphones present different frequencies to each ear. Isochronic tones can work without headphones.
