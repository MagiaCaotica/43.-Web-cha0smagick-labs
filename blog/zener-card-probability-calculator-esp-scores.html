Frequently Asked Questions

What is the probability of guessing a Zener card correctly by chance?

With 5 symbols, chance is 1/5 = 20% per card. Over 25 cards, chance expectation is 5 correct. The distribution follows binomial probability with n=25 and p=0.2.

What Zener card score is statistically significant?

A score of 9/25 (36%) has a p-value of approximately 0.03 — only 3% probability by chance. This is statistically significant. Scores of 7-8 (28-32%) are suggestive but not conclusive.

What is psi-hitting and psi-missing?

Psi-hitting is scoring above chance (e.g., 10/25). Psi-missing is scoring below chance (e.g., 1/25). Both indicate non-random performance. Psi-missing may indicate ESP operating in reverse.
